

## Commands Learned
- Why this matters in the field:
This exact pattern — strings [binary] | grep [pattern] — is called `static analysis.` You extract information from a file without executing it.

Used for:

-Malware analysis — find C2 IPs, hardcoded credentials
-Firmware analysis — extract embedded passwords from router firmware
-Binary reverse engineering — first recon step before disassembly
-CTF challenges — exactly what you just did
